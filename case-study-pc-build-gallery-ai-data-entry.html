Case study

PC Build Gallery AI Data Entry

PC build content needed to be added to a gallery quickly and consistently. I built an AI-assisted workflow that reduces manual data entry while improving the quality and presentation of uploaded build images.

Book My Free Discovery Call Read the business problem
IndustryRetail and PC hardware
Company sizeGrowing SME
DepartmentContent and operations

Overview

Manual gallery publishing became a guided AI workflow.

The client needed a faster way to prepare PC build gallery entries without sacrificing accuracy, visual quality, or consistency.

The business problem

Each PC build required repeated manual cleanup before publishing.

Staff had to identify parts, enter build details, prepare images, format content, and keep the gallery consistent across many submissions.

Manual data entry

Build specs had to be typed or cleaned up repeatedly before publishing.

Inconsistent entries

Gallery quality depended on who prepared the content and how much time they had.

Image preparation

Photos often needed enhancement or cleanup before they were ready to represent the build.

Challenges

The system needed to improve content quality without adding more admin work.

  • Varied input qualityBuild information and photos could arrive in different formats and quality levels.
  • Accuracy mattersIncorrect parts or specs would damage customer trust.
  • Visual presentationThe gallery needed images that made completed builds look polished.
  • Repeatable publishingThe workflow needed a consistent structure for every build entry.

Solution

An AI-assisted data entry and image enhancement workflow.

The system helps extract or structure PC build information, prepare gallery-ready content, enhance uploaded images, and surface entries for review before publishing.

The workflow keeps a human review step where accuracy matters while removing the repetitive formatting and cleanup work.

System architecture

From raw build content to gallery-ready entries.

The workflow connects upload, AI-assisted extraction, image enhancement, review, and publishing.

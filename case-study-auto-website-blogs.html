Case study

Auto Kachi Blogs

The client needed a more consistent way to publish relevant tech content for SEO. I built an AI-assisted workflow that researches current technology news, drafts content, and prepares posts for review and publishing.

IndustryTechnology content and ecommerce
Company sizeGrowing SME
DepartmentMarketing and content

Overview

A slow content process became a repeatable SEO publishing workflow.

The system helped the client turn relevant technology news into review-ready website content without manually researching and drafting every article from scratch.

The business problem

Consistent SEO content required too much manual research and writing.

Publishing useful content takes research, topic selection, writing, formatting, internal linking, and review. Without a system, the process becomes inconsistent.

Manual research

Staff had to monitor technology topics and decide what was worth writing about.

Inconsistent publishing

Content output depended on available time instead of a reliable workflow.

SEO opportunity cost

Relevant topics could be missed or published too late to support traffic growth.

Challenges

The workflow needed speed without low-quality AI content.

  • Source qualityThe system needed to work from credible topics and avoid low-value content scraping.
  • Brand relevanceNot every tech news item was useful for the client's audience.
  • Human reviewAI could assist drafting, but the business still needed editorial control.
  • SEO formattingPosts needed structure, titles, excerpts, and publishing metadata.

Solution

An AI-assisted content pipeline for research, drafting, and publishing.

The workflow identifies relevant tech topics, prepares a draft in the client's content style, formats the article for the website, and routes it for review before publishing.

The system was designed to support SEO operations without removing editorial judgment or copying full articles from other publishers.

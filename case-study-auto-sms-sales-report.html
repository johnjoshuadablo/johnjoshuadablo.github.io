Case study

Automated SMS Sales Report

Key people needed timely visibility into sales performance without waiting for manual reports. I built an automated SMS workflow that sends sales, gross, net, and margin percentage updates to decision-makers.

Book My Free Discovery Call Read the business problem
IndustryRetail and operations
Company sizeGrowing SME
DepartmentLeadership and finance

Overview

Manual sales updates became automated executive visibility.

The system helped key people receive consistent performance updates without waiting for staff to prepare and send numbers manually.

The business problem

Sales performance updates were too dependent on manual reporting.

Leadership and shareholders needed regular visibility into total sales, gross, net, and margin percentage. Manual updates created delay and repeated admin work.

Delayed visibility

Decision-makers had to wait for reports or ask for updates.

Repeated manual work

Staff had to calculate, format, and send recurring performance summaries.

Inconsistent communication

Different people could receive updates at different times or in different formats.

Challenges

The report needed to be timely, accurate, and simple to read.

  • Reliable calculationsTotal sales, gross, net, and margin percentage needed consistent business logic.
  • Recipient controlThe right people needed the right updates without exposing unnecessary information.
  • TimingReports needed to run on schedule without staff intervention.
  • Short formatSMS messages needed to be concise enough for quick leadership review.

Solution

A scheduled SMS reporting workflow for sales performance.

I created an automation that gathers sales data, applies reporting calculations, formats the summary, and sends SMS updates to key people and shareholders.

The focus was not another dashboard. The focus was making the right numbers reach the right people without manual chasing.

System architecture

From sales data to scheduled SMS visibility.

The workflow connects source data, calculations, message formatting, recipient rules, and SMS delivery.

Leadership received clearer recurring performance updates.

  • Consistent formatRecipients received the same core numbers in a repeatable structure.
  • Better operating rhythmSales, gross, net, and margin updates became part of a dependable reporting cadence.
